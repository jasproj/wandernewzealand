About WanderNZ

We connect travelers with local guides, raft crews, charter captains, and outfitters across New Zealand — direct to the operator, no booking-platform markup.

Our Mission

WanderNZ started with a simple problem: the best operators in New Zealand often get lost in noise. The family-run rafting crew on the Shotover, the kayak guide who runs the Bay of Islands at first light, the small-boat operator working Milford Sound off the main jetty — they often get buried under listings that pile booking fees and price markups on top of the operator's actual rate.

We built WanderNZ to fix that. The platform indexes 500 verified tours from Auckland down to Queenstown and across both islands, and links travelers directly to each operator's booking page. No markup on our end. No middleman fees stacked between you and the guide.

When you book through WanderNZ, your money goes to the person actually running the tour. That's how tourism in New Zealand is supposed to work.

What We Believe

The principles that guide everything we do

🥾

Local First

Every operator we list is based in New Zealand. No mainland-owned aggregators selling Kiwi adventures from offshore, no global tour packagers pretending to be Kiwi.

💵

No Markup

Our links go to operator booking pages at operator prices. We earn a referral commission from completed bookings — it comes from the operator's marketing budget, not from your wallet.

🌿

Conservation & Track Care

We prioritize operators who follow DOC (Department of Conservation) guidelines, brief travelers on biosecurity at trailheads, and don't overload sites like the Tongariro Crossing or the Routeburn.

📝

Real Listings

Tour descriptions come from the operator's actual booking page. No AI-generated marketing copy, no recycled stock blurbs.

📍

North + South, Both Matter

Auckland to Stewart Island — both islands have their own pace. We don't push you toward a single "top" region or pretend everywhere's just Queenstown.

🤝

Local Economy

Every booking through WanderNZ is money flowing to a guide, a raft crew, or a small charter outfit. That's the point.

Transparency Matters

WanderNZ is a discovery platform — not a booking site, not a tour operator. When you click Book Now, you're sent directly to the operator's booking system to pay them in NZD or USD on their terms.

We earn a referral commission from some bookings, which keeps the platform running. The commission comes out of the operator's existing marketing budget. It does not affect what you pay.

Affiliate Disclosure

WanderNZ participates in referral programs with New Zealand tour operators. When you book through our links, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. This helps us maintain the platform and continue listing local operators. We only list operators we'd recommend to friends visiting the country.
