About this site

WanderNZ is operated by Claduta Corp. The site is a tour-discovery platform — it indexes verified local New Zealand tour operators and links travelers to those operators' booking pages. WanderNZ is funded through affiliate commissions on completed bookings; the model is explained in detail under "Affiliate Disclosure" below.

How bookings work

WanderNZ does not book tours directly. When you click "Check Availability" on a tour listing, you are sent to the operator's booking page (powered by FareHarbor). At that point:

  • The operator's own terms and conditions apply to your reservation
  • The operator collects your payment and contact details
  • The operator confirms your booking and issues any tickets or vouchers
  • WanderNZ is not part of the booking contract between you and the operator

Affiliate disclosure

WanderNZ earns commissions on tour bookings completed through FareHarbor's affiliate program. The affiliate code walktheplankadventures appears in every booking URL on the site. This code routes commissions through a partner LLC arrangement; this does not affect the price you pay — operators set their rates independently of the commission.

We tell you this upfront because we believe travelers deserve to know how the sites they use stay in business.

Where Amazon product links appear on WanderNZ (occasionally in blog content, packing guides, and similar), the site is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program. If you click an Amazon link and make a purchase, WanderNZ may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you.

Accuracy of tour information

Tour details — descriptions, prices, photos, schedules, availability, ratings — are provided by FareHarbor and the individual operators. WanderNZ indexes this information but does not independently verify each tour's accuracy. Prices can change. Availability changes constantly. The operator's booking page is always the authoritative source.

Age requirement

To book a tour through the operators we link to, you must be 18 or older (or have a parent/guardian with you who is 18+). Individual operators may have additional age, height, or fitness requirements for specific activities — always check the operator's booking page before reserving.

Refunds, cancellations, weather changes

Refunds and cancellations are handled by the individual operator according to their own policy. WanderNZ does not process refunds because we don't collect payment. If a tour is cancelled due to weather, mechanical issues, or other operator decisions, the operator is responsible for the refund or rebooking — contact them directly.
